About the Idea
Brief About the Idea — Why AI is Required
Bridging the agricultural knowledge gap for 120M+ Indian farmers through multi-agent AI and voice-first multilingual design
🚨 The Problem
  • 120M+ farmers lack timely access to expert agricultural guidance
  • Information on diseases, prices, schemes is fragmented across platforms
  • Language barrier — most solutions are English-only; farmers speak 22+ languages
  • Low digital literacy — complex apps are unusable for rural farmers
  • No personalization — generic advice ignores local soil, weather, crop context
  • Delayed response — crop diseases spread while waiting for expert visits
💡 Our Solution: KrishiSaathi
  • Multi-Agent AI — 5 specialist agents orchestrated by a Supervisor Agent
  • Voice-first design — speak in any Indian language, hear back in the same
  • RAG-powered knowledge — 50K+ verified agricultural documents for accurate answers
  • Persistent memory — remembers farmer context for personalized advice
  • Real-time data — live weather, market prices, and scheme updates
  • AWS-native architecture — Bedrock, Polly, Transcribe, RDS, EC2
🧠 Why AI is Required
Traditional approaches cannot solve this at scale
AI enables real-time, personalized, multilingual agricultural guidance at a cost of ₹0.50/query vs. ₹500+ for an in-person expert visit. Multi-agent architecture provides specialized domain expertise that no single model can match. RAG ensures factual accuracy while LLMs provide natural conversation.
